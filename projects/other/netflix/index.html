Specification

Write a program to win the Netflix Prize.

Ignore the qualifying data. It's just here for explanation.

Just use the probe data, and see if you can meet or beat their RMSE of 0.9474.

Input

Training Data:

  • 17,770 movies
  • 480,189 customers
  • about 100,000,000 ratings
  • about 5,600 ratings per movie
  • about 200 ratings per customer

Qualifying Data:

  • 2,836,401 ratings, no customers from training data

Probe Data:

  • 1,425,333 ratings, all customers from training data
